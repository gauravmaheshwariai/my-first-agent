Krishi and the Sleepy Stars

One night, Krishi could not sleep at all. She tiptoed outside and sat under the big mango tree in her garden. The stars were out, and the night was soft and quiet.

Then she heard it. Hoo hoo. Hoo hoo. Up in the mango tree sat Ollie, a very old and very wise owl. He looked down at her and blinked his big golden eyes.

Krishi whispered, why are you still awake, Ollie? The old owl ruffled his feathers and smiled. He said, I was just about to ask you the very same thing, little one.

Ollie said, you know what I do when I cannot sleep? I count all the things that made me happy today. Krishi thought hard. She said, my puppy licked my nose this morning, and that made me laugh.

Ollie said, yes yes, hold that happy thing close, like a little warm blanket around your heart. Krishi closed her eyes and smiled. She could almost feel the laugh still sitting there inside her chest.

Krishi yawned a big sleepy yawn. Ollie whispered, hoo hoo, there it is. Off to bed now, little one. Krishi tiptoed back inside, and this time, she fell fast asleep in no time at all.
